About Us

LonDance is Orange County's premier dance Studio with the highest quality instructors that can make any dance style fun, fast and easy! At LonDance, learning any partner dance such as Rumba, Salsa, Swing, Waltz, Tango and countless more has never been more exciting. Our hand-chosen, certified instructors will ensure that each of your lessons are fun, comfortable and crafted to meet your goals.


LonDance Studio has been operating in Orange County for over 30 years and has been the bench mark for ALL other studios in Southern California. What sets LonDance apart however, is that we specialze in building confidence by welcoming you into our friendly, family atmosphere and giving you the skills and abilities to dance in any setting. Whether it's an anniversary party, wedding, cruise, or just a fun night out, dancing at LonDance guarantees you the ability to look like a star in any social setting. We have students wth an enormous range of personal goals.


Aside from the fun social family that LonDance provides, all of our teachers are also poised and ready to make any average dancer into a fierce competitor. Being top studio with top teachers and top students at hundreds of Dancesport events for several decades, LonDance is world reknown for producing extremely high quality dancers in every category in all levels. Livng up to it's legendary reputation year after year. No wonder LonDance is the premier studio in Orange County.


Whatever your goals, with our fun, friendly staff, along with all of our wonderful students, we will make you feel right at home.

What We Offer

Private Lessons

There's no faster way to master the dances you love than with the one-on-one instruction received through Private lessons. No matter if you're a couple or single individual, privates guarantee you 100% of your instructor's attention. Learn More >

Group Classes

Group classes are the perfect opportunity to learn new patterns, techniques and dance styles...plus meet new, like-minded people who share your passion for Dance! LonDance offers nightly groups Monday-Friday Learn More >

Social Parties

If by midweek, you're thinking, "All I Want to Do is Dance," then LonDance's Wednesday night Practice Parties are the perfect solution. Our parties are open to the public and everyone from beginner to advanced is welcome. Learn More >

Showcases

If you're ready for your "Close Up", then you've come to the right place! LonDance's showcases gives you the opportunity to let your inner self become a shining Star! Each of the showcases feature an overall theme, such as our upcoming "Night at the Oscars".Learn More >

Wedding Dances

You've been dreaming about it...Spent countless hours planning it, wondering how you'll feel...Now, your wedding day is almost here and even the smallest detail seems like a huge deal! Learn More >

Team Matches

There's no quicker way to improve your skills than by challenging yourself to compete, and Team Matches are the perfect place to start! In a Team Match, you and your instructor will compete against your peers--other students in your age division, skill level and also dance style. Learn More >

Competition

Competitons are electrifying events that allow you to showcase your talent, while competing against other amateurs! In Competition Dancing, you and your teacher will compete in the categories of American Style and/or International Style. Learn More >
